## Design Intent
CH Health OS should feel like:

- A luxury medical spa
- A private concierge
- A calm, intelligent system
- Premium, not clinical
- Elegant, not cold

This is not a fitness app.
This is not a hospital system.
This is personal infrastructure.

---

## Visual Principles

1. Calm over stimulation
2. White space is a feature
3. Fewer elements, higher meaning
4. No visual guilt or pressure
5. Everything should feel intentional

---

## Color Direction (Guidelines)
- Base: warm whites / soft ivories
- Accents: muted greens, soft stone, light gold
- Alerts: neutral emphasis (never red unless critical)
- Success: subtle affirmation, not celebration

Think:
- Boutique wellness clinic
- Scandinavian + Japanese spa
- Modern private medicine

---

## Typography
- Serif or soft humanist sans for headlines
- Clean sans-serif for body text
- Large line height
- Comfortable reading

No techy fonts.
No condensed fonts.
No aggressive weights.

---

## UI Components Tone
- Cards > lists
- Sections > feeds
- Timelines > logs
- Gentle dividers
- Minimal icons

---

## Motion & Feedback
- Subtle transitions
- No confetti
- No shaking
- Feedback should reassure, not excite

---

## Gamification Visuals
- No points explosions
- Badges are understated
- Rewards are described in words, not icons

Visual reward = calm satisfaction
