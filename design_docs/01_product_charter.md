# CH Health OS – Product Charter

## Purpose
Create a private, family-centered health operating system that maximizes
daily adherence, long-term consistency, and preventive health outcomes
through routines, habits, and intelligent feedback loops.

## Target Users
- Individual users: Candy, Héctor
- Group entity: Family CH

## Core Principles
1. Rules > AI
2. Adherence beats optimization
3. Health decisions must be traceable and explainable
4. Low friction daily usage (Telegram-first)
5. Family motivation without pressure or guilt

## Success Metrics (MVP)
- Daily routine adherence > 75%
- Weekly streak continuity
- Consistent weekly body composition input
- Telegram daily engagement ≥ 1 interaction/day

## Out of Scope (MVP)
- Oura API integration (v1)
- Labs, biomarkers, and retest automation (v2)
- Travel / jet lag logic
